Open Father's Day: Fenders on Front Street Car Show

MULE Expedition Outfitters will be OPEN 9AM-4PM, Father's Day, June 15th for the Fenders on Front Street Car Show in Downtown Issaquah!

Our Suzuki LJ81 is entered in the car show and will be parked along Front Street as entry #126. Joe will be there handing out cards and free decals, as well as being available to chat about "The Zook."

Find Us Here!

Dana will be at the store (same building as the XXX Root Beer Drive In), where the car show parade ends later in the afternoon. We have several Father's Day promotions and sales going on, so drop in and see us, and check out the car show, if you are in town that weekend.
